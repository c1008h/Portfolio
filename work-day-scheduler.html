Workday Scheduler

Workday schedulers are great for seperating your worklife and your personal life apart.

A quick click, and a quick glance will have you up to date on your everyday tasks.

Having to constantly check your calendar and figuring out which event is for your personal life and which one is for your work life can get a bit confusing. This work day scheduler is made to make everything organized. Everything will be storaged to your local storage of your browser. With one click on the link while you're working on your desktop, you'll be able to see what needs to be done for the rest of your shift.
